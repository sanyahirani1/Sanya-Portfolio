MA Dissertation · Featured Project

Everyone has a story.
Not everyone tells it the same way.

A creative documentary born out of my internship at Arts Uplift — a charity that brings elderly people together through art and creativity.

"For some period you are alone — but not always. You eventually end up finding your people."

The project explores how loneliness transforms into connection — through words, painting, movement, or music.

Documentary FilmCommunity StorytellingElderly WellbeingArts Uplift UKIn Progress · 2026

Theme

Loneliness, human connection, and the many languages of self-expression — verbal, visual, and creative.

Methodology

Documentary filmmaking, community interviews, and observational storytelling within Arts Uplift sessions.

Key Insight

Introverts and extroverts both have rich inner worlds — they simply need different doors to share them through.

Progress

Filming underway · Expected completion 2026
