Featured Project

Everyone has a story.
Not everyone tells it the same way.

My MA dissertation is a creative documentary project born out of my work at Arts Uplift — a Birmingham charity that brings elderly people together through art and creativity.

"For some period you are alone — but not always. You eventually end up finding your people."

The project explores how loneliness transforms into connection when people are given a space to express themselves. Some share their stories through words. Others through painting, movement, or music. The film captures both — honouring the fact that being introverted doesn't mean being silent.

Documentary Film Community Storytelling Elderly Wellbeing Arts Uplift UK MA Dissertation In Progress 2026

Project Theme

Loneliness, human connection, and the many languages of self-expression — verbal, visual, and creative.

Methodology

Documentary filmmaking, community interviews, and observational storytelling within Arts Uplift sessions.

Key Insight

Introverts and extroverts both have rich inner worlds — they simply need different doors to share them through.

Progress

Research & filming underway — expected completion 2026
